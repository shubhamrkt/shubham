Details About Shubham Stationary Are Listed Here

Shubham Stationary was established in 1975 by Mr Sitaram and is managed by him. He started his journey as a small retailer of stationery and art supplies in South Extension, New Delhi. In a few years of his business tenure, he witnessed the growing demand of international products in India and was contacted by artists, architects, designers, students, and other clientele that expressed their interest in high quality art supplies (difficult to avail in the country at the time). The same led to engaging conversations with importers, where he shared with them his vision and broad perspective of the Indian demand for art supplies. His investment in market development and expansion of an international product range eventually led to what we call “a One Stop Shop” for Art & Design enthusiasts. In 1993, he began to nurture his vision of this One Stop Shop, to not just cater to the needs of (his) clients all over India via an extensive product range but also create an experiential environment with technological initiatives that would transform the art supply business in India. With exchange of ideas and intellectual conversations with his son, Karan, Mr.Shubham established Shubham Enterprises, Lajpat Nagar in 2010; India’s biggest art & stationery store managed by Mr. Karan Dev Arora. Determined towards success, Shubham Enterprises, in a year of opening, incorporated multiple international and domestic brands that focused on the growing needs of its consumers. The year 2017, was a milestone year for Shubham Enterprises, and got the ball rolling. With competitive pricing, abundant availability of international brands (White Nights, Stillman & Birn, Golden, Williamsburg, Derwent and others) and special discounts (356 days of the year); SITARAM (an initial brandname) became a choice, for many! Currently, Shubham Enterprises is one of the biggest import and distribution companies for art supplies in India, striving to provide excellent services and a quality shopping experience to customers at any given time. Our goal is to make your shopping experience convenient, affordable and enjoyable. Keeping the same in mind, we hold true to consistently monitoring competitive market pricing while building on prospects for future expansion. .show class.
